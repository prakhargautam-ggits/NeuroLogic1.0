NLP Challenge Tracks
Pick Your Challenge

Choose one track. Build your model. Submit before 10 PM IST. You may only attempt ONE challenge.

Challenge 01 · Beginner–Intermediate
C1
Real-Time Disaster Tweet Classification

Build a binary classification model that identifies whether a tweet refers to a real disaster or not. Enables first responders to prioritise critical situations at scale.

METRIC: MACRO F1-SCORE Dataset → train.csv + test_nolabel.csv · Kaggle: NLP with Disaster Tweets
Challenge 02 · Intermediate
C2
Fake News & Misinformation Detection

Develop a robust NLP model to classify news articles as real or fake based on title and content. Combat the spread of misinformation at scale with AI.

METRIC: OVERALL ACCURACY (%) Dataset → challenge2_train.csv + challenge2_test_nolabel.csv
Challenge 03 · Advanced
C3
Multilingual Toxic Comment Classification

Build a multi-label classifier identifying toxicity across multilingual text — threats, obscenity, insults, and identity-based hate across global platforms.

METRIC: MEAN ROC-AUC SCORE Dataset → Jigsaw Multilingual Toxic Comment · 6+ Languages
